Our Mission

Female Empowerment

We want to create a supporting community for women in tech and in other related fields. At Prodeva, you will get insprired by other sucessful females through their stories as well as their journey on how they get there. You can also post your story to inspire other people. Beyond that, you will get supported when you feel lost in your career by connecting to our professionals to tell them your story and listen to their advices. You can also choose to join our mentorship program where you will be match with a mentor who can assist you with everything you need to achieve your dream.

Education

Technology is one of the most popular field nowsaday. If you ever want to try learn to code, but you do not where to start, we got you! Prodeva provides crash courses for many different programming languages from which you can choose from. We will match you with a mentor to help you learn. We will also guide you from your first line of code to your fisrt sucessful project. Prodeva will have different topics from which you can choose to work on as your personal projects. Furthermore, you will also have an opportunity to work in group project to improve your collaborating skills and learn from the others.

Community

Prodeva helps you to connect to other females in your local, so you can build a real relationship out of our plaform. We will host monthly workshop where you will garther with other local female to make friends, study together, expand your network, and share career opportunity. Everything gets easy when you have some partners and a supporting community.

Get on the Ferris Wheel

Let's Get Started

Elwyn Brooks White once observed, “I see nothing in space as promising as the view from a Ferris wheel.” Let's get your journey begin and get on the Ferris wheel of knowledge. You will be amazed by how much you will learn and what you can do when you get to the top.

Mentor Matching

A mentor can be just the right person to get you started on the path to your dreams. Get qualified advice from someone who has been in your shoes! They can offer career support, be a sounding board, or connect you with valuable opportunities you may have never heard of. Our Mentor Matching program ensures that you are paired with someone who has been exactly where you are, and who has your best interests in mind. Click on the Mentorship tab to get started!

Learn to Code

Now that technology is at the forefront of jobs in nearly every field, there has never been a better time to develop some coding skills. With so many different languages, you’re bound to find something that suits your taste! However, it might be overwhelming to figure out how to get started. Here at Prodeva, we’ll help you every step of the way, offering resources to ensure that you never get stuck.

Personal Project

Personal Project is a great way to showcase the skills you have developed over the years. This project accentuates your approach to learning, and application of your major into an area or topic that interests you. It is an excellent opportunity to produce a truly personal and create a product/outcome to demonstrate a consolidation of your learning in the major/career path you are considering.

Group Project

Throughout the mentorship program, you will have the opportunity to work in group projects and solve programs together. Prodeva will offer a diversity of topics that groups can choose to work on on a weekly basis. This is a great way to pool your ideas and see problems from different perspectives. The purpose of our group projects is to ensure that students have the opportunity to engage in critical skills for processing information, and evaluating and solving problems as well as management skills. Teamwork is one of the most vital components of your professional development.

Community

Community plays an important role in professional learning and development. The community here at Prodeva helps you connect to other females to build and connect relationships across our platform. Our monthly workshops will enhance your networking opportunities, and this is a perfect way to make friends, collaborate together, and share your career opportunity. With a supporting community, Prodeva serves to motivate and inspire us all to do more and strive to perform our best.

Giving Back to Community

The best part of working with Prodeva is that you’ll always be part of a bigger community. You’ll be able to give back to your community by becoming a mentor yourself! It’s always a fulfilling feeling to know that you’re helping people out the same way you had help.
